About Us

Váli-Bitcoin is a cutting-edge payment processing company that leverages the power of the Bitcoin and Lightning networks to provide merchants with a fast, secure, and efficient point-of-sale solution. With our advanced technology, we aim to tackle the problems that traditional point-of-sale solutions can’t.

Our team consists of experts in both the Bitcoin and Lightning networks, as well as payment processing, ensuring that we stay ahead of the competition and provide the best possible solutions to our customers. Our ultimate goal is to help merchants save time, reduce friction, and enhance the overall customer experience while ensuring maximum security and privacy.

We believe in the power of these technologies and are committed to educating and promoting the use of Bitcoin and Lightning networks, as well as providing merchants with the tools they need to be successful in this rapidly changing industry.
