Intro to Lightning

Peer-to-peer payments are made using the Lightning Network. To allow very instantaneous and inexpensive settlement of bitcoin between participants, it makes use of payment channels anchored on the Bitcoin blockchain. In order to distribute payments to everyone in the network without requiring participant confidence, several similar payment channels are linked together.
